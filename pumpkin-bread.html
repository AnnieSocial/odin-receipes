Pumpkin Bread

Moist Delicious Pumpkin Bread

This pumpkin bread is a moist, delicious fall treat that will keep you coming back for more.

Ingredients:

Wet ingredients in mixer:

  • 1 (15 oz) can pumpkin puree
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup coconut oil
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla

Dry Ingredients whisked together:

  • 3 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tablespoons cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ginger

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In mixer bowl combine pumpkin, eggs, melted coconut oil, vanilla, water and sugar.
  3. In separate bowl combine remaining dry ingredients and whisk together.
  4. Combine both mixtures and pour into pan. Set timer for 40 minutes and check with toothpick or fork. Add time as needed.
