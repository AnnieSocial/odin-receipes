Amazing Rich Lasagna

The italian sausage in this amazing dish really gives it flavor above and beyond the usual bland, flavorless beef in most lasagnas.

Ingredients:
  • 2 lbs italian sausage
  • 2 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 Tbsp basil
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 4 cups tomatoes
  • 3 cups tomato paste
  • 2 1/2 cups lasagne noodles
  • 6 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1 Tbsp + 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 2 lbs mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Oven: 375 degrees

  1. Brown meat slowly, spoon off fat.
  2. Add garlic, basil, salt, tomatoes and tomato paste.
    3. Simmer uncovered for 30 minutes. Stir occasionally
  3. Cook noodles in boiling salted water, drain and rinse.
  4. Mix ricotta, parmesan, parsley, eggs, salt and pepper.
  5. Layer noodles (1/2), half of the ricotta filling, half of the mozarella, half of the meat sauce then repeat layers.
  6. Cook at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
