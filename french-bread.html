French Baguettes

This Recipe is a Sure Favorite

This easy recipe is a breeze to throw together and will become a fast family favorite. The simplicity of the recipe makes it easy to throw together for perfect baguettes every time!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups water
  • 5 cups bread flour
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp yeast
  • 2 egg yolk
  • 2 Tbsp water

Directions:

Oven: 375 degrees

  1. Place water, bread flour, sugar, salt and yeast into bowl and mix with dough hook.
  2. When thoroughly mixed place dough in a greased bowl. Cover and let rise in warm place for 30 minutes or until double. Dough is ready if indentation remains when touched.
  3. Punch down dough. On a lightly floured surface, roll into a 16x12 inch rectangle. Cut dough in half creating two 8 x 12 rectangles. Roll up each half of dough tightly, beginning on 12 inch side, pounding out any air bubbles as you go. Roll gently back and forth to taper end.
  4. Place 3 inches apart on a greased cookie sheet. Make deep diagonal slashes across loaves every 2 inches or make one lengthwise slash on each loaf. Cover and let rise in a wam place for 30-40 minutes or until doubled in bulk.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Mix egg yolk with 1 tablespoon water; brush over tops of loaves.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
